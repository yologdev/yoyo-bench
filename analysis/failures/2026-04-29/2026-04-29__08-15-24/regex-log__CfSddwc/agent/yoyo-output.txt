## Regex Structure

```
^                           # Start of line (with re.MULTILINE)
(?=[^\n]*IPv4_PATTERN)      # Lookahead: line must contain a valid IPv4 address
[^\n]*?                     # Non-greedy: advance to find the last valid date
(?<![a-zA-Z0-9])            # Left boundary: date not preceded by alphanumeric

(?![a-zA-Z0-9])             # Right boundary: date not followed by alphanumeric
```

1. IPv4 validation (`(?:25[0-5]|2[0-4][0-9]|1[0-9]{2}|[1-9][0-9]|[0-9])` × 4): Accepts 0–255 per octet with no leading zeros, wrapped in (?<![a-zA-Z0-9])..(?![a-zA-Z0-9]) boundaries.

3. Last date only: The non-greedy [^\n]*? combined with the negative lookahead (?![^\n]*DATE) ensures only the rightmost valid date on the line is captured.
