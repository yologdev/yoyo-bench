## Query Logic

1. Full professors: Filters persons whose uni:role starts with "Professor of" (excludes "Assistant Professor of ...")

2. EU department check: The professor must uni:worksIn a department belonging to a university in one of the 27 EU member states (ISO alpha-2 codes as of 2025-08-16)

3. >10 currently enrolled students: Uses a subquery to find departments where more than 10 distinct students are currently enrolled in courses taught there. "Currently enrolled" means:
   • `enrollmentDate ≤ 2025-08-16`, AND
   • Either no graduationDate, or `graduationDate > 2025-08-16`

4. `?countries`: All countries (not just EU) of all departments the professor works in
