A subquery groups students per (professor, department) in EU universities, counting only students who are currently enrolled: `hasEnrollmentDate ≤ 2025-08-16 AND (no hasGraduationDate OR hasGraduationDate > 2025-08-16). The HAVING` clause keeps only departments with > 10 such students.
