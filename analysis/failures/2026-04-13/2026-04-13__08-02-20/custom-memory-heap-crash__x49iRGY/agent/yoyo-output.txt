Then when ::operator delete(nodeptr)` is called in the destructor, g_custom_heap is null, so it falls through to `std::free() — but the memory was allocated from the custom heap arena (not from std::malloc`), causing the crash.

The fix: in user.cpp, we need to ensure the custom heap is NOT active (i.e., g_custom_heap == nullptr) when _Facet_Register_impl runs, OR ensure the custom heap lives long enough past exit handlers.
