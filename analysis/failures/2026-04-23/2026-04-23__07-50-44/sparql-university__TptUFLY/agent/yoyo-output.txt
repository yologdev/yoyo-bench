## Query Logic

1. Full professors — uses `FILTER (CONTAINS(?role, "Professor") && !CONTAINS(?role, "Assistant"))` to match roles like "Professor of X" but exclude "Assistant Professor of X"

2. EU department filter — checks that the professor `uni:worksIn a department (uni:belongsTo) a university (uni:locatedInCountry`) one of the 27 EU member states (ISO alpha-2 codes). This excludes `CH (Switzerland/ETH Zurich) and US` (MIT/Berkeley).

3. >10 currently enrolled students — uses a subquery that:
   • Traverses `?dept ← uni:isTaughtIn ← ?course ← uni:isEnrolledIn ← ?student`
   • Filters for students with `enrollmentDate ≤ 2025-08-16 AND (no graduation date OR graduationDate > 2025-08-16`)
   • Groups by department and applies HAVING COUNT > 10

4. Countries — collects all countries across all departments the professor works in (not just EU ones)
